BOSS Token

The Central part of our economy

The BOSS Token will be initially airdropped to NFT holders. Further supply is created as a staking reward for locking up your BOSS Bull (incentivizing hodlers). Additional supply is earned in our P2E (Play To Earn) Game. Users will use BOSS to pay for upgrades, mint new NFTs on special candy machines, and more. Swaps and liquidity pools will be available on Saber and Radium.
