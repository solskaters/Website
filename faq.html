General Questions

1. What are Subway Skaters ?

Subway Skaters is a collection of 6,666 uniquely generated Skateboards boarding freely on Solana Skate park! Our love for Skateboards has finally driven our team to launch this project.

2. Who are the Creators behind Subway Skaters ?

The Subway Skaters collection is the ultimate effort & labour of the few but passionate individuals in our team who are Gaming & Nft enthusiasts!

3. When is the Launch ?

12th December , at 19:00 UTC

4. How much is it to mint a SKATEBOARD ?

The mint price for Skateboards is free (Only gas fees)!

5. If I miss the mint, how can I purchase a Skateboard ?

You can purchase from Secondary Markets like Magic Eden, Solsea, Digital Eyes & Alpha Art.

6. What are the benefits of being Whitelisted ?

Whitelisted people will be able to Mint 10 Minutes prior to public Mint at 18:50 UTC.

7. How many Whitelist Spots are There ?

ONLY 200.

8. How can I get Whitelisted ( Early Access) ?

By successfully Inviting 5 people.

9. When will the Rarity Released ?

Rarity will be released on Moonrank & Solana NFT Rarity Once we're Minted!
